GET UP AND GO

Why Pokémon GO?

Collections

Collect more than 600 kinds of Pokémon from five regions and go on adventures with your favorite buddy!

Battles

Battle and catch various gym raid bosses, compete with players from all over the world, and beat the Team Go Rocket leader!

Friends

Send gifts to friends, increase friendship levels, send them invitation to Ex-Raids, trade Pokémon, and have a friendly battle!

NEW BUDDY SYSTEM NOW LIVE IN POKÉMON GO
Medals are sort of achievements earned by Trainers for reaching certain milestones, like distance walked, PokéStops visited, Pokémon caught.
Each medal has three levels: bronze, silver, and gold.
For achieving some of the medals Trainers are extra rewarded with bonuses - such as an increased chance of successfully catching certain Pokémon type or bonus wardrobe pieces for Trainers are unlocked to be purchased.
Click on the shadow medals below for more information!
